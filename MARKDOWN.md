-   item1
-   item2
    -   subitem 1-1
    -   subitem 1-2

        -   subitem 1-2-2

### Ordered Lists

1. first
2. second
    1. third
    2. four
        1. five
        2. six

### Inserting Images

![alt](URL 'title')

-   alt takes the place of the image if it cannot be diplayed
-   parenthesis contains image source
-   title in quotes indicates the text to display when the mouse hovers over the image (can be omitted)
